1. Planet around an iron star

Not a habitable-world story so much as a last-world story.
Think: a dark, frozen planet orbiting a barely relevant gravitational ember in a universe where the sky is almost empty. No bright sunrise, almost no natural energy income, civilizations surviving off black-hole engineering, tunneling reactors, or ancient stored mass. This is great for:

end-of-time archaeology
civilizations living on dwindling inheritance
metaphysical dread: there is no frontier left, only duration

The vibe is not “planet under a weird sun.” The vibe is “planet after suns.”
